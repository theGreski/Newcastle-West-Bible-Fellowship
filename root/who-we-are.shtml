What is a Bible Fellowship
We are a group of ordinary people who believe that the Bible is the living Word of God and is real to us today.

We are people who live in and around Newcastle West who come from all age groups and walks of life. We have been meeting as a church since 1999.

Jesus Christ has made a difference in our lives and our trust is in Him alone for the forgiveness of our sins.

We enjoy being together and welcome new friends to our local church to explore the Christian faith with us.

YOU would be very welcome to come along and find out more for yourself.

Mission

We believe that it is our responsibility, as believers in the Lord Jesus, to share the good news about who He is with the whole world.

We endeavour to model our personal and church life on the teaching of God's Word rather than on personal opinions or religious traditions, and so seek to follow the priorities established in the early church, including the apostles' doctrine, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayer.

Our elders

Elders - Leadership of an assembly is the responsibility of a plurality of elders (Titus 1:5), also described as overseers (1 Timothy 3:1-2; Titus 1:5-7). Elders are responsible to shepherd the people of God (1 Peter 5:2; Acts 20:28). This involves feding (Acts 20:28), guarding (Acts 20:31), supporting (Acts 20:35), guiding by exhortation (Titus 1:9), and example (1 Peter 5:3; Hebrews 13:7). Ministries in which the elders should be primarily involved include teaching, visitation and counseling.
